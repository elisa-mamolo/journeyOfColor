THE JOURNEY OF COLOR


IBA presents the Journey of Colour, a wonderful activity for everybody who wishes to have a fun, colourful and crazy day together with friends, family and the community in Kolding!
Lots of fun and people will share a day together to mix sport, passion and of course COLORS!
The Journey of Colour is a 5km running route through the main streets of Kolding, where we will all be celebrating love, unity and happiness. We will finish this journey with our Yoga party, where we will experience these same feelings through a calming and restorative session
