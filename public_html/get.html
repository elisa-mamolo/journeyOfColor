WHERE IS IBA?

The location

5km Run

The journey of colour is taking place in the Kolding city center. The starting and the finishing point of the run is at the IBA. You can see the location on Google maps here

Yoga Party

The yoga party will take at Alykkeskolen, only a few minutes' walk from IBA. You can see the location on Google maps here. Remember to be on time, or you will miss out on the painting fun!

Public Transport

Kolding is pretty well connected to other cities by other countries. The train station is actually only 1km away from IBA. There are also buses from other cities stopping directly in front of IBA. You cannot use accessibility for an excuse to not attend this event! What are you waiting for? Plan your journey right here, right now.

Parking Possibilities

If you are travelling to Kolding by car, don't worry - there are plenty of places you can leave your car while you are joining us on the Colour Run. There are parking places around IBA, and up at Venstreringgade near Alykkeskolen.
