FAQ



I still don't understand what is a 'Colour Run' and a 'Yoga Party'.
Well, let's keep it simple! A colour run is a simply 5km running route all around the city, where the participants are showered with different coloured powders and paints at the different stops. The participants will also get packets of coloured powder which they can use to shower themselves and their friends.
A yoga party is exactly that! It is a gathering of people who will do yoga together in a fun party atmosphere.
The theme is still colour, so participants will have access to glow paints and glow sticks to help build the party atmosphere!
What is the inspiration behind the name'A journey of colour'?
This journey of colour is both a physical and spiritual journey. We will run all around Kolding, exploring the city through the different colour stations. Our yoga party will be a spiritual journey of medidation - we will be focusing on the different colour of our Chakras, and how to balance our Chakras through different yoga poses.
I've never run a 5km before, can I still attend?
Yes! This run is for absolutely everybody who would like to participate! Today, we don't care about speed, time or performance; it's all about having a crazy, fun day with friends, family and other people living in Kolding! So run, jog, walk, and enjoy your day!
How can I prepare for this race?
Like we mention, this race is not about performance, so you shouldn't worry about how fast you run, just focus on enjoying it! However, if you really want to show up and ace it, you can follow our Couch to 5K guide.
Do I need special shoes?
Hell no! Just regular sneakers will do fine. We don't expect anyone to spend thousands of kroner on professional running shoes! But, if you DO already have professional running shoes, because you're already a super cool professional runner, then go ahead and put them on. Just to warn you, we will be looking at your colour stained shirts, and not your shoes.
I paid 100kr for a ticket, so what is included?
The 100kr ticket is a basic ticket, which means that you can participate in the race, get one package of coloured powder, and access to the yoga party. You can get more information about the different packages in the Buy Now section.
Where do I get my participant kit?
We start the race at IBA, and this is also where you get your participation ticket. There will be a stand near the school entrance, where you can show your QR code, and get your sticker and your extras; powders or T-shirt.
Can someone else pick it up for me?
Sure, as long as they have a copy of the confirmation email with the QR code on it!
I've changed my mind, I don't want to attend. Can I refund my ticket?
Yes, you can refund your ticket up to a week before. Read our Terms and Conditions in the Buy Now section for more information.
Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
No, each registration is tied to one name and email address. You cannot transfer your ticket, but you get cancel and get a refund up to a week before the event.
Are the paints used in the colour run and yoga party safe?
Yes, we care about our and your health- we have ensured that we only use Food Grade colourings in our powders and paints. We can guarantee that these colours are not toxic or harmful.
Can children attend these events?
If you promise to be the most awesome mum or dad ever, then you can bring your children to this event. You can walk, run or push your stroller along the 5km route, but please remember to join in the fun and throw some coloured powders at each other.
Will the colours ruin my clothes and shoes?
The colours are in powder form, and our Colour Blowing Station at the end of the route will remove most of the powder. Not all of it though. As with any other stains, the faster you wash dirty clothes, the easier it is to remove the stain.
Is the colour run a timed race?
Hell no! We don't care about the time, we just want people to have fun. So you can run, walk, take a break, drink a beer, have a paint war, and keep going until you get to the end station.

Anything is possible if you put your mind to it! You are very welcome to join the yoga party, even if you didn't participate in the run. Just make sure to show up at the right place and at the right time, so you can get your paints, yoga mat and find a good place in the hall.
I've never done Yoga before, can I still join the party?
Absolutely! This yoga event is not about putting you legs behind your head. There is no need for previous experience with yoga to participate in our Yoga Party. Anyone is welcome, male or female, flexible or stiff.
Is the yoga challenging / physically intense?
We thought that after an exhausting day of running around and throwing paint, nobody would want to go through a challenging and rigorous yoga class. We decided therefore to keep it calm and slow, and focus more on meditation and relaxation.
What style of yoga will be practiced?
This yoga will be a mix of various slow and calming yoga styles, to work towards opening and balancing our Chakras.
Are the yoga instructors certified?
Yes, our two yoga teachers are fully qualified to teach the specific yoga styles which will be practiced in the session.
What do I need to bring for the yoga party?
We have some mats available, but if you have your own mat you are very welcome to bring it with you. Otherwise, we believe bringing your own water bottle and positive energy is enough.
What happens if it's raining?
We're in Denmark, it's always raining! But we won't let that stop us! The show must go on, unless we're dealing with an unimaginable amount of water.
