What is Color Yoga about?

Color Yoga is a yoga session connected with wonderfully glowing in the dark neon color paints. The event will be based on 2 sessions (1h each) with 30 mins break (before each session) to have some fun with the paints and make our body glowing. First session starts at 16.30, another one at 18.00. Don't worry! This part of the IBA Sport Day is for everyone, the yoga intensity will be adjusted to the group skills. Believe us, even if yoga isn't really your thing, you should definitely try these glowing paints!

What kind of Color Yoga is that?

IBA Sport Life is aware that not everybody here is a proffessional trainer. That's why during IBA Color Yoga we will be practicing Hatha Yoga. It is a general yoga style, relatively gentle, slow and great for beginners or students who prefer more relaxed form of yoga. After all, we are here to combine fun and physical activity, aren't we? Another reason to join us - it will be also a great way to stretch your body after a long run!
